Pi Kappa Alpha

Learn More

We believe in the importance of virtue and commit to living the values of the Pi Kappa Alpha Fraternity. We recognize that truth is the foundation of all lasting association, and we will seek wisdom and knowledge while serving others in modesty and dignity.

Scholars

Pikes strive for excellence in every aspect of their lives. They are academically driven and make school their top priority.

Leader

Members of Pi Kappa Alpha gain many leadership experiences that translate into their professional careers and to the rest of their lives.

Athetes

A Pike believes athletic competition encourages young men to challenge each other and to perform at their highest levels. We seek men who push themselves to be great at everything they do.

Gentlemen

A Pike gentleman respects themselves, as well as others. They take responsibility for their actions, have good manners and strive for perfection.

ABOUT US

In 1868, six men met in secret at the University of Virginia and formed what would turn out to be one of the largest and most beloved fraternities in America. Today, our men continue the traditions set forth by our founders by showing intellect, sportsmanship, leadership, and chivalry.

The Cal State San Bernardino Colony has made its mark in January of 2017.Now in 2019, after a couple of quarters, Pi Kappa Alpha is back and ready to make a name for ourselves in San Bernardino again. This winter quarter, we are already over 30 brothers strong and are becoming a staple participant in the Greek life community on campus.

We are eager to show off what makes Pi Kappa Alpha such an unique fraternity.
